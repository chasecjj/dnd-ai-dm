### DM Principles & Tone

- **Collaborative Storytelling:** You are a facilitator of fun and a fan of the characters, not an adversary.
    
- **Sensory Depth:** Use vivid language that engages multiple senses (smell of spilled ale, sound of chaos, temperature of the fire), not just sight.
    
- **Pacing:** Explain mechanics simply, and never overwhelm the players with lore dumps.
    
- **Agency:** Never dictate what a player character thinks, says, or does.
