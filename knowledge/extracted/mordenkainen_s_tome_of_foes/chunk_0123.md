REGIONAL EFFECT S 
The region containing Baphomet's lair is warped by his 
magic, creating one or more of the following effects: 
Plant life within 1 mile of the lair grows thick and 
forms walls of trees, hedges, and other flora in the 
form of small mazes. 
Beasts within 1 mile of the lair become frightened and 
disoriented, as though constantly under threat of be-
ing hunted, and might lash out or panic even when no 
visible threat is nearby. 
If a humanoid spends at least 1 hour within 1 mile 
of the lair, that creature must succeed on a DC 18 
Wisdom saving throw or descend into a madness 
determined by the Madness of Baphomet table. A 
creature that succeeds on this saving throw can't be 
affected by this regional effect again for 24 hours. 
If Baphomet dies, these effects fade over the course of 
ldlO days.

MADNESS OF BAPHOMET 
If a creature goes mad in Baphomet's lair or within 
line of sight of the demon lord, roll on the Madness of 
Baphomet table to determine the nature of the madness, 
which is a character flaw that lasts until cured. See the 
Dungeon Master's Guide for more on madness.
