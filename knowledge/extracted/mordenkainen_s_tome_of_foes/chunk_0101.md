If your DM allows the use of feats from chapter 6 of the 
Player's Handbook, your deep gnome character has ac-
cess to the following special feat. 
S v1RFNEBLIN M AGIC 
Prerequisite: Gnome (deep gnome) 
You have inherited the innate spellcasting ability of your 
ancestors. This ability allows you to cast nondetection 
on yourself at will, without needing a material compo-
nent. You can also cast each of the following spells once 
with this ability: blindness/deafness, blur, and disguise 
self. You regain the ability to cast these spells when you 
finish a long rest. Intelligence is your spellcasting ability 
for these spells.
