## Description
Stink Alley is a narrow, trash-filled passage choked between the looming warehouses. A single, sputtering oil lamp casts long, distorted shadows, revealing piles of refuse and the slick sheen of questionable fluids on the cobblestones. The air hangs thick with the mingled scents of rotting fish, stale ale, and unwashed bodies.
