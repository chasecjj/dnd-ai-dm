## Description
Dead Man's Pinch is a claustrophobic alley choked with refuse and the stench of stale ale and decay. The slick cobblestones underfoot are perpetually damp, reflecting the meager light filtering down from the tall buildings on either side. Rats scurry in the shadows, their eyes glinting in the gloom.
