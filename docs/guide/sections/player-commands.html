Your Spellbook of Commands

Every command at your disposal

Command Example What It Does
!roll <formula> !roll 1d20+5 Roll dice through Foundry VTT

!recap !recap Shows the latest session recap

!pc <name> !pc Frognar Look up a character's stats
/whisper /whisper Opens a private thread for secret actions
