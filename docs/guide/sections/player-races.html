Your race shapes who you are — your heritage, your strengths, your story

Human
Versatile and ambitious. Humans adapt to any situation.
The most widespread race in the realms, driven by endless ambition and remarkable adaptability.

Racial Traits:

  • +1 to all ability scores
  • Extra language of your choice

Elf
Ancient and graceful, with eyes that see through darkness.

  • Darkvision (60 ft.)

Dwarf
Stout, stubborn, and built to endure.

  • Darkvision (60 ft.)
  • Dwarven Resilience (poison resistance and advantage on saves vs. poison)
  • Stonecunning (History bonus for stonework)

Halfling
