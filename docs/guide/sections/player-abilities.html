The Six Pillars of Power

Every hero is defined by six fundamental ability scores

Strength
Physical might.

Governs melee attacks, carrying capacity, and Athletics checks.

"Break down the door, grapple the troll, climb the cliff."

Dexterity
Agility and reflexes.

Governs ranged attacks, AC, initiative, Acrobatics, and Stealth.

"Dodge the fireball, pick the lock, land the arrow."

Constitution
Endurance and vitality.

Governs hit points and concentration saving throws.

"Survive the poison, march all night, hold the spell."

Intelligence
Knowledge and reason.

Governs Arcana, History, Investigation, Nature, and Religion.

"Decipher the runes, recall the lore, spot the clue."

Wisdom
Perception and insight.

Governs Perception, Insight, Medicine, Survival, and Animal Handling.

"Sense the ambush, read the liar, track the beast."

Charisma
Force of personality.

Governs Persuasion, Deception, Intimidation, and Performance.

"Charm the guard, inspire the troops, bluff the dragon."

Your modifier = (Score − 10) ÷ 2, rounded down. This modifier is what you add to dice rolls. Saving throws use these same modifiers — they represent your ability to resist effects like poison (CON save), dodging a trap (DEX save), or resisting a charm spell (WIS save).
