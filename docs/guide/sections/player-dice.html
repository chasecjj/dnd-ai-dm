The d20 is king. Nearly every check, attack, and save starts with rolling a d20 and adding your modifier. The higher the result, the better you perform.

Rolling Dice

Use the !roll command to roll dice through Foundry VTT:

Command Example What It Does
!roll <formula> !roll 1d20+5 Roll dice through Foundry VTT
!roll <formula> <reason> !roll 1d20+5 Perception Roll with a label

Common Formulas

Formula When You Use It
1d20+5 Standard ability/skill check
2d6+3 Damage roll
1d20+7 Attack roll
4d6kh3 Roll 4d6, keep highest 3 (ability score generation)
2d20kh1+5 Advantage (roll 2d20, keep highest, add modifier)
2d20kl1+5 Disadvantage (roll 2d20, keep lowest, add modifier)

Advantage & Disadvantage

Advantage means roll 2d20, take the higher result. Disadvantage means take the lower. Circumstances in the game grant these — a clever plan might earn advantage, while fighting blind imposes disadvantage.

Wait for the prompt. Don't pre-roll — the DM and AI decide what rolls are needed and will ask you when it's time. Rolling without being asked won't count.
